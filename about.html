About Me

Developer / Designer

Majoring in software engineering, often writes code. Passionate about frontend development and UI/UX design.

Experience

Hello, my name is Satriyo, better known as Riyo because it’s easier to call me that way.

Since childhood, i was always curious about technologies and the creative expression you can do using them like video editing, photoshop, digital music production and stuff among those lines. On the first year of middle school, i entered a graphic design extracurricular. Naturally, i was pretty good at it. Directed by my teacher, i used Corel Draw Suite to design some stuff like banners, posters, and characters.

In other aspects, i started studying software development around 2nd year of middle school. I was interested on how websites, applications and interfaces we see in our daily lifes were created. I spent my weekends studying website development from the beginning, starting by learning HTML and CSS from a 6 hours course i found on youtube.

Entering vocational high, i began taking software development seriously by keeping on learning and creating projects to enhance my skills relating to those subjects. I still consider myself as a beginner and will continue on improving, keeping up with the rapid growth of technology.
